Please enter a location and click search to find population density estimates for your favorite restaurants!

Motivation:

The pandemic has effected us all, especially us Foodies! However, it is important to stay safe and continue social distancing. Therefore, in an attempt to help local restaurant owners and foodies like us find safe locations for takeout and avoid foot traffic, we have developed this web app.

Santa is probably hungry after delivering all those gifts, so lets help him feast while social distancing!

How to use?

1. Enter a location you are interested in eating at (be sure to click one of the suggestions to confirm your location).

2. Enter any optional parameters and press Search!

3. The application will retrieve data from nearby locations and calculate estimated probability density. process may take some time depending on the number of restaurants in a given location, so please be patient!

4. Have fun exploring and finding a safe place to get food! Stay safe!
